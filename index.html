Olympic Robot Competition:

Shoot penalties, Save other robots' penalties, Shoot darts on a target, solve a maze and collect balls of a certain color from a field of balls of different colors. We spent many days and nights working on this project and we ended up getting third place out of seven teams. Here is a photo with the cool people I worked with.
